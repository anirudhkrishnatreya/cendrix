Healthcare FAQs

Are your specialists trained on HIPAA requirements?

Yes. Every Cendrix healthcare specialist completes mandatory HIPAA certification before onboarding to any client. We maintain current training records and conduct annual refresher programs.

Which EHR systems do your specialists work in?

Our specialists are trained across major platforms including Epic, eClinicalWorks, Athenahealth, Kareo, DrChrono, Practice Fusion, and others. We can also train on proprietary systems during onboarding.

How quickly can we onboard a specialist?

Typical onboarding is 5–10 business days. This includes HIPAA documentation, system access provisioning, workflow training, and a supervised go-live period.

Do you sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA)?

Yes. A signed BAA is a standard part of every engagement before any PHI access is granted. We also review client-specific BAA addendums.

What time zones do your specialists cover?

We support US Eastern, Central, Mountain, and Pacific time zones, as well as UK GMT/BST. Extended coverage and overnight support is available.
